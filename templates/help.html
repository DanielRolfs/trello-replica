How to use

  1. To create a new Task under go to "Add Task"
  2. After pressing the "CREATE TASK" button the Task will appear in the "Backlog" section.
  3. The assigned User can now take the task, by clicking on the Task in the "Backlog".
  4. The task is now moved from the "Backlog" section into the "Board" section.
  5. In the "Board" section, you can see the Progress of every Task.
  6. To bring the Task to the next level click and hold it and move it per Drag & Drop in the next level.
  7. In every section you can open the task by clicking on it.
If you need help or there are any problems contact us.
