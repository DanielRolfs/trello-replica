How to use

  1. To create a new Task use the "Add Task" section.
  2. After pressing the "CREATE TASK" button the Task will appear in the "Backlog" section.
  3. The assigned Users can now take the task, by clicking on the "to Board" button.
  4. The task is now moved from the "Backlog" section into the "Board" section.
  5. In the "Board" section, you can see the Progress of every Task.
  6. To bring the Task to the next level click and hold it and move it per Drag & Drop in the next level.
  7. In every section you can open and edit the task by clicking on it.
  8. To delete on Task use the Trah symbol.
  9. You can use the filter- oder searchfunktion at the bottom left side.
  10. Hover oder the symbol. In the Popup you can set a filter.
  11. for searching press the loop symbol.
  12. To reset the filter or search funktion use the "RESET FILTER" button at the bottom right side.
If you need help or if there are any problems contact us.
